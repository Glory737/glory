About Glory

Meet the artist behind your perfect look

My Story

With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, Glory has built a reputation for creating stunning, natural-looking makeup that enhances each client's unique beauty. What started as a passion for makeup artistry has evolved into a full-time career dedicated to making women feel confident and beautiful on their most important days.

Glory specializes in bridal makeup, having worked with over 500 brides, and has expanded her expertise to include editorial work, special events, and personalized makeup lessons. Her attention to detail, understanding of different skin types, and ability to adapt to each client's vision sets her apart in the industry.
